Want to get rid of e-mail tsunami ?

Start by disabling "send immediately"

Just hit send, and store them in your Outbox. Before you close the application, hit F9, and send them. You still have the chance to review them before sending. It's a kind of respect for your readers. Any mail sent is handled with care.

You are in control of the sending time. You bulk send them at specific times. You don't receive random short answers, you don't start a conversation over e-mail anymore. It doesn't work. Use a chat application or get a room.

Plus you GTD.
