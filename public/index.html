Want to get rid of e-mail tsunami ?

Use your outbox

Start by disabling "send immediately".

Just hit send, and store your e-mail in your Outbox.
Before you close your mailclient or at the end of the day, review your e-mails and hit F9 to send them. It's a kind of respect for your readers. Any e-mail sent is handled with care.

You are in control of the sending time.
You bulk send them at specific times, so you won't receive chat-like short answers, you won't start a conversation over e-mail anymore. It doesn't work. Use a chat application or get a room.

Respect your readers time

Skip salutation. No signoff.
Skip e-mail signature. It's a waste of time.
